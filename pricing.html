Is Game DJ still in beta — and how do I get a licence?
Yes, Game DJ is still in beta and improving fast. Two ways to get a licence: subscribe the normal way through Paddle (inside the app), or — if you'd rather sort it out personally during the beta — email gamedj@waveric.fi and we'll set you up.
How do I actually buy it?
From inside the app. Download it, use the 14-day trial, and when you're convinced, click Buy a license in the app. That opens a secure Paddle checkout, and the app activates itself as soon as the payment clears — no keys to copy.
What happens when the trial ends?
Game DJ stops playing until you subscribe. Nothing is deleted — your categories, sounds, cue points and rosters are all still there, exactly as you left them, waiting.
Can I get a refund?
Yes. There's a 14-day money-back guarantee on your first payment, on top of the free trial that came before it. Email us and it's done — see the refund policy.
Who am I actually buying from?
Game DJ is made by Waver Corp Oy, a Finnish company. Payments run through Paddle, which acts as the merchant of record — they handle the transaction and the VAT, and they're who appears on your card statement.
